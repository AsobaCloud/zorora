## Motivation

The synthesis code for outline generation and section-by-section expansion already exists in synthesizer.py (outline_sections config: [4,6]) but run_deep_research bypasses it at line 1047 and goes straight to direct synthesis. This produces flat answers, not structured reports.

Add research_type-specific section templates (e.g., trend analysis: context/drivers/data/implications/outlook). Ensure synthesis output has markdown heading structure matching the outline.

## Acceptance Criteria
Deep research output has 4-8 named markdown sections with heading structure. Each section cites its own relevant sources. Output reads as a structured research report, not a flat answer.
