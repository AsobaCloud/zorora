# SEP-073: Chart generation from structured data in deep research reports

**Status:** Open
**Created:** 2026-03-28
**Project:** zorora

## Summary
Generate matplotlib charts from Zorora's own structured data (SAPP DAM prices, Eskom generation, FRED commodities, World Bank indicators) and embed them in deep research output alongside text synthesis

## Motivation
Published research articles include 4-7 data visualizations per piece (EU energy mix trends, mineral intensity ratios, capacity projections). Text-only reports cannot match this quality bar. Zorora already has 64+ time series and feasibility analysis generates matplotlib charts (SEP-045). The data and charting capability exist but are not connected to the deep research output pipeline. Visual-first output is core to Zorora's product vision as a domain-specific Claude Code for renewable asset owners.

## Proposed Change
When market context is injected into deep research (SEP-070), also generate matplotlib charts for the most relevant time series. Use LLM-generated chart specifications (title, series, date range, chart type) based on the research query. Render charts as base64 PNG images embedded in the synthesis markdown. Reuse the matplotlib charting patterns from feasibility analysis (SEP-045). Support line charts (time series), bar charts (comparisons), and stacked area charts (composition).

## Acceptance Criteria
Deep research reports include 2-4 embedded charts generated from Zorora's structured data. Charts are contextually relevant to the research query. Chart types include line (trends), bar (comparisons), and stacked area (composition). Charts render in the web UI alongside synthesis text.
