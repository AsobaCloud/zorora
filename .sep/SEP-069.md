# SEP-069: Enable content fetch and increase synthesis token budget

**Status:** Open
**Created:** 2026-03-28
**Project:** zorora

## Summary
Activate the existing ContentExtractor (SEP-005) for top sources and increase the synthesis content budget from 5,000 chars to 15-20K chars so the reasoning model receives full-text evidence with specific data points, quotes, and statistics

## Motivation
Published research articles cite specific statistics (e.g. 'EU oil share dropped from 39% to 28%', 'China controls 90% of REE processing'). These data points live in the full text of source documents. Zorora's synthesis prompt currently receives ~5,000 chars of 300-char snippets — the model cannot see the numbers it needs to make quantitative claims. The ContentExtractor exists (SEP-005) but is disabled in config.

## Proposed Change
Enable content_fetch in config (enabled=True). Increase SYNTHESIS content_budget from 5,000 to 15,000 chars. Increase max_sources_for_content from 20 to 30. Fetch full text for top non-academic sources. Restructure the evidence prompt to include longer extracts (up to 1,000 chars per source) from fetched content, prioritized by relevance and credibility score.

## Acceptance Criteria
Synthesis prompt includes full-text extracts (not just snippets) from top sources. Content budget is 15K+ chars. ContentExtractor runs for top 20-30 sources. Synthesis output contains specific data points and statistics sourced from fetched content.
