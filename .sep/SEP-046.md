# SEP-046: BESS Scoring, Diligence Domains, and Synthesis

## Summary
Add BESS-specific site scoring factors, diligence query decomposition, and synthesis pipeline with storage-oriented domains and charts (DAM price profiles, revenue estimates, tariff waterfalls).

## Motivation
The existing diligence pipeline is designed for brownfield generation asset acquisition. BESS projects have fundamentally different economics: revenue comes from arbitrage spreads and peaker displacement rather than PPAs, performance is measured in round-trip efficiency and cycle life rather than capacity factor, and grid connection requirements differ (4-quadrant inverter, reactive power). BESS-specific scoring and diligence domains ensure the analysis asks the right questions.

## Change Description

### BESS Site Scoring
Extend `tools/imaging/site_score.py` with BESS scoring factors:

| Factor | Source | Weight |
|---|---|---|
| Arbitrage spread (DAM peak − off-peak) | SAPP DAM data | High |
| TOU spread (applicable Eskom tariff) | Eskom tariff data | High |
| Grid proximity (distance to MTS substation) | GCCA GeoPackage | Medium |
| RE penetration (solar+wind MW in zone) | GEM + GCCA | Medium |
| Demand level (supply area load) | Eskom demand data | Medium |
| Peaker competition (OCGT/pumped storage nearby) | Station Build Up | Low |

### BESS Diligence Domains
Add `decompose_bess_diligence_query()` to `engine/query_refiner.py` with 6 storage-specific domains:

1. **Revenue Model** — Bankable annual revenue from DAM arbitrage + TOU + peaker displacement
2. **Grid Connection** — Substation capacity, voltage, transmission zone, connection requirements
3. **Tariff & Charging Cost** — Cost to charge, applicable tariff schedule, seasonal patterns
4. **Regulatory & Licensing** — NERSA storage licensing, grid code for BESS, export license
5. **Market Structure** — SAPP DAM vs bilateral trading, cross-border opportunities, market reform
6. **Risk Assessment** — ZAR/USD exposure, tariff escalation, competing storage, demand outlook

### BESS Diligence Synthesis
Add BESS synthesis prompt and chart generation to `workflows/deep_research/synthesizer.py`:

- BESS analyst system prompt (storage investment advisory persona)
- Per-section analytical questions for each domain
- Source routing by domain (same pattern as brownfield diligence)
- Quality gate for BESS diligence output format

### Charts
- 24h DAM price profile (peak/standard/off-peak shading, high vs low season)
- Monthly arbitrage revenue estimate (low/mid/high scenarios, seasonal overlay)
- Eskom TOU tariff waterfall (peak → standard → off-peak by season)

### Integration Points
- Discovery view: [Score BESS] button on MTS zone popup triggers BESS scoring
- Scouting BESS tab: cards scored with BESS factors
- Scouting Feasibility: Trading tab uses BESS-specific analysis when item_type is 'bess'
